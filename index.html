Scout’s note

James, your brief points clearly toward understated elegance for professional settings. You want something that rewards those who know, without announcing itself to those who do not. All three picks share that quality. The Longines is our firm top pick for its technical depth at this price; the Frederique Constant is the value outlier worth serious consideration; and the Tudor offers the strongest long-term hold if resale ever matters to you.

Our three recommendations
Top pick
Longines

Master Collection Moonphase

£2,350  new, authorised dealer

Powered by Longines calibre L678, a COSC-certified movement accurate to within a few seconds per day. The moonphase complication adds genuine horological depth without ostentation, perfectly suited to a boardroom environment. At 40mm with a slim 9.3mm profile, it sits elegantly under a shirt cuff. Available on a hand-stitched alligator leather strap or a five-link steel bracelet. Case is stainless steel with a sapphire crystal and exhibition caseback. Recommended retailer: Watches of Switzerland.

COSC certified Exhibition caseback Moonphase complication
Frederique Constant

Classics Business Timer

£1,895  new, authorised dealer

Powered by FC-270 in-house calibre with a 42-hour power reserve and an AM/PM indicator at 6 o’clock. A rare complication at this price point and a genuine conversation piece. The silver-toned dial features applied baton indices and a date window at 3 o’clock. At 40mm, it sits within your preferred case size. Available on a dark brown leather strap with a deployment clasp. Manufactured in Plan-les-Ouates, Geneva. Recommended retailer: Mappin & Webb.

In-house movement Best value pick Geneva manufacture
Tudor

Black Bay 36

£2,730  new, authorised dealer

Powered by Tudor MT5402, an in-house manufacture movement with a 70-hour power reserve and chronometer certification. The 36mm case is stainless steel with a domed sapphire crystal and a burgundy bezel insert that gives it subtle character without flamboyance. Offered on a leather strap, fabric strap, or steel bracelet. Water resistant to 200m. The Black Bay 36 has demonstrated strong secondary market performance and is considerably easier to acquire than its Rolex stablemates. Recommended retailer: Goldsmiths.

Manufacture movement 70hr power reserve Strong resale value
