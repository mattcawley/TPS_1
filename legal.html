Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 May 2026

Timepiece Scout is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle it.

What we collect

When you submit a report request, we collect your name, email address, and the preferences you share in the questionnaire. If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address only. We do not collect payment information directly; any payments are processed via secure third-party providers.

How we use your information

We use your information solely to produce your personal watch recommendation report and to communicate with you about it. We will not use your details for unrelated marketing without your explicit consent. If you subscribe to our newsletter, you will receive occasional horological writing and may unsubscribe at any time.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may use trusted service providers (such as email delivery platforms) to help operate our service; these providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and may not use your data for their own purposes.

Data retention

We retain your report request data for up to 12 months, after which it is securely deleted. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the address below.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. You also have the right to object to processing and to data portability. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@timepiecescout.co.uk.

Cookies

Our website uses only essential cookies necessary for it to function. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. No personal data is collected through cookies.

Contact

For any privacy-related queries, please contact us at hello@timepiecescout.co.uk. We aim to respond within five working days.
